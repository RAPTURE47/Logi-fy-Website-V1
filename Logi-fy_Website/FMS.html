Freight Management System

Solution Designed to streamline and optimize the various processes involved in the Transportation and Management of International Freight (AIR/SEA and EXPORT/IMPORT). This system is particularly valuable for logistics and supply chain companies, helping them manage the movement of goods efficiently.

Here are key features and functionalities of a typical Freight Management System:
  • Order and Shipment Management
  • Route Planning and Optimization
  • Carrier Management
  • Inventory Visibility
  • Document Management
  • Real-time Tracking and Monitoring
  • Rate Management
  • Analytics and Reporting
  • Billing and Invoicing
  • Compliance and Documentation
